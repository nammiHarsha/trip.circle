About This Trip

This isn't just a trip — it's an expedition into one of the last wild frontiers. Sail through Komodo National Park, come face-to-face with dragons, snorkel untouched reefs, and explore remote islands few travellers ever reach. From sunrise hikes at Padar to swimming with manta rays and cruising across turquoise waters — this journey is raw, rare, and unforgettable.

Brief Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival in Labuan Bajo + Sunset
Arrive in Labuan Bajo and settle into your stay before heading to the harbor for a stunning sunset. The evening sets a calm start for the adventure ahead.
Day 2 - Start Liveaboard Cruise + Snorkeling
Board your liveaboard and sail into Komodo waters, surrounded by dramatic island views. Your first snorkeling stop introduces the rich marine life of the region.
Day 3 - Komodo Dragons + Pink Beach + Manta Point
Trek through Komodo terrain to see the famous dragons, then head to Pink Beach for a unique coastal stop. Later, snorkel at Manta Point where ocean life is at its best.
Day 4 - Padar Sunrise + Drive to Riung
Begin before dawn for the iconic Padar sunrise hike with panoramic island views. After descending, continue overland toward Riung through scenic Flores routes.
Day 5 - 17 Islands Snorkeling + Drive to Ende
Explore the Riung 17 Islands marine zone with crystal-clear snorkeling stops and island views. In the second half, drive onward to Ende for the next phase of the journey.
Day 6 - Kelimutu Lakes Sunrise + Beach
Head out early to witness sunrise at the extraordinary Kelimutu crater lakes. Later, slow the pace with a relaxed beach stop and peaceful coastal views.
Day 7 - Cultural Exploration Day
Spend the day discovering local villages, viewpoints, and lesser-known corners of Flores. It is a great chance to connect with the culture and enjoy the route at a richer pace.
Day 8 - Fly to Bali + Party Night
Fly to Bali and shift from expedition mode to celebration mode for your final evening. Enjoy the nightlife and close the trip with the group on a high note.
Day 9 - Departure
After checkout, depart with a camera full of island scenes and unforgettable marine encounters. The journey ends, but the expedition memories stay with you.
