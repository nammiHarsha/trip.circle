Thailand Full Moon Trip

 

Duration 8 Days
Group Size 15–20 people
Stay 3/4 star hotels
Trip Type Group Trip

About This Trip

This is one epic where the party doesn't seem to end! In between all the partying, we do some cool island hopping. Along with all the swimming, snorkelling or just chilling on the boats, we also visit some highly popular spots where famous Hollywood movies have been shot! So pack your beachwear, put on your sunglasses and maybe some neon for the FM?

Brief Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival in Phuket
Touch down in the vibrant island kingdom of Phuket and transfer to your beachside hotel. In the evening, stroll the electric Bangla Road on Patong Beach — neon lights, open-air bars, street food stalls, and live music create an irresistible first impression of Thailand's legendary nightlife.
Day 2 – Old Town & Big Buddha
Start the day at leisure or opt to do some water sports. Later, visit the iconic Big Buddha, pictures at the old town, and watch the sunset by the beach chilling with the group.
Day 3 – Phuket to Koh Phangan
After breakfast, depart Phuket and journey by ferry to Koh Samui — the spiritual epicenter of the world-famous Full Moon Party. Check into your beach hotel, shake off the travel dust with a refreshing dip, and enjoy a relaxing Thai spa session before heading to Chaweng Beach for fire shows.
Day 4 – Full Moon Party — The Night of a Lifetime
Before heading out in the evening, hop on a snorkelling boat tour around the beautiful beaches of Koh Phangan. In the evening, paint your bodies neon because we are heading to The Full Moon Party!
Day 5 – Koh Phangan Island Exploration
With the Full Moon behind you, Koh Phangan reveals its quieter soul. Catch up on some sleep or if you are partly hung over, rent a scooter and wind through jungle roads to elevated viewpoints for panoramic island vistas. Those who are still partying, you know what to do!
Day 6 – Onward to Krabi — Mangroves & Limestone Karsts
Arrive in Krabi, check in and launch straight into the outdoors. Kayak through the tranquil mangrove canals of Ao Thalane. Unwind with a traditional Thai massage along Ao Nang Beach followed by a fresh seafood dinner at the waterfront.
Day 7 – Phi Phi Islands Expedition
Embark on a full-day speedboat expedition to the legendary Phi Phi Islands. Dive into vibrant coral reefs teeming with tropical fish and marine life, enjoy the beachside lunch and we return to Krabi around sunset.
Day 8 – Departure
A final Thai breakfast, and a chance to pick up handmade keepsakes from the market. Transfer to the airport (Krabi or Phuket) and depart with memories of fire-lit beaches, open ocean, and the full moon's golden path across the sea.
