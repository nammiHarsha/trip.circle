Indonesia (Flores)

 

Duration 9 Days
Group Size 15-20 people
Stay Hotel + Boat
Trip Type Group Trip

About This Trip

Carefully curated group experience designed for exploration, culture, and meaningful travel connections.

Brief Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival in Labuan Bajo
Arrive in Labuan Bajo and check into your hotel before easing into the destination. If time allows, visit Rangko Cave, then enjoy a beautiful sunset to kick off the expedition.
Day 2 - Komodo Cruise
Board your liveaboard and begin island hopping through stunning Komodo waters. The day includes snorkeling in clear reefs before spending the night aboard under open skies.
Day 3 - Komodo and Pink Beach
Trek in Komodo National Park to spot dragons in their natural habitat, then continue to the iconic Pink Beach. Later, snorkel at Manta Point before returning to your overnight boat stay.
Day 4 - Padar + Ende + Moni
Start with the famous Padar sunrise hike, then fly to Ende to continue your Flores overland route. Visit Wologai village and a nearby waterfall before resting in Moni.
Day 5 - Kelimutu + Riung
Visit the surreal Kelimutu crater lakes in the morning, then stop by Blue Stone Beach for a unique coastal view. Continue driving to Riung for the overnight stay.
Day 6 - 17 Islands
Take a private boat through the 17 Islands area with snorkeling stops and relaxed beach time. After the marine experience, continue onward for your overnight stay in Bajawa.
Day 7 - Bajawa + Ruteng
Explore Bena village and experience local heritage before unwinding at natural hot springs. Later, enjoy a local Arak tasting and continue to Ruteng for the night.
Day 8 - Return + Bali
Visit the famous spider web rice fields and a scenic waterfall before your flight to Bali. End the day with a vibrant beach club evening and an overnight stay in Bali.
Day 9 - Departure
Enjoy a final breakfast before checkout and departure. Your trip closes with a rich mix of island cruising, culture, and overland adventure memories.

What's Included?

  • Accommodation: 8 Nights of hotel, guesthouse & liveaboard boat stays throughout the journey.
  • Transport: Private AC car, private driver, and all overland transit as per itinerary (except in Bali).
  • Tickets & Permits: Entry tickets to all listed destinations and national park permits included.
  • Activities: Private boat and snorkelling equipment for the Riung 17 Islands activity. Local guides for all treks and tours.
  • Meals: Breakfast daily. Lunches and Dinners included on cruise and overland days, except where explicitly noted.
  • Extras: Mineral water throughout the entire road trip portion of the expedition.
  • Flights:
    • - India → Labuan Bajo (international)
    • - Labuan Bajo → Ende (Day 4)
    • - Labuan Bajo → Bali (Day 8)
  • Airport Transfers: TukTuk/Taxi to the starting hotel in Labuan Bajo (Day 1) & return from Bali (Day 9).
  • Specific Meals:
    • - Day 1: Lunch & Dinner
    • - Day 4: Lunch during the flight
    • - Day 8/Bali: Lunch & Dinner
  • Optional Activities: Scuba diving on the Komodo cruise (available for certified divers at additional cost).
  • Personal Expenses: Alcohol, snacks, tips, party expenses (Finns Beach Club / Motel Mexicola), and transport within Bali.
  • Insurance: Comprehensive travel insurance is mandatory and must be arranged independently before departure.

Important Notes

  • Swimming is mandatory
  • Travel insurance required
  • Remote stays may be basic
  • Weather may affect schedule
