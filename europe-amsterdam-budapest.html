About This Trip

Four of Europe's most iconic cities. One legendary journey. Cruise Amsterdam's canals, rave in Berlin, pub crawl through Prague, and soak in the thermal baths of Budapest — a full-blown Euro adventure packed with iconic landmarks, electric nightlife, hidden gems, and memories that will last a lifetime.

Brief Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival in Amsterdam
Arrive in Amsterdam, check in and explore your first evening in one of Europe's most liberating cities – let’s start the evening (when it is still young and not hazy!) with a free drink on the house! and explore the best kept secrets of Amsterdam!
Day 2 – Water, Culture & Freedom
We start the day with a Canal Cruise drifting through Amsterdam's UNESCO-listed canal ring aboard a glass-topped boat. Later we head to the legendary Heineken Experience — learn the brewing process, discover the brand's 150-year history, and enjoy a couple of complimentary beers as part of the experience.
Day 3 – Wind & History
Today we go on a day trip to Zaandam & Zaanse Schans: Escape the city to this open-air museum village where traditional Dutch windmills, wooden clog workshops, and cheese farms paint a vivid picture of Holland's rural heritage
Day 4 – Amsterdam to Berlin
Transfer to Berlin by the intercity train. Freshen up and experience Berlin's nightlife which is legendary and genre-defining — from techno temples in repurposed power stations to intimate jazz cellars and rooftop bars. The city truly comes alive after dark.
Day 5 – Berlin Exploration
We start the day with a Walking Tour with Local Guide: Navigate Checkpoint Charlie, the Brandenburg Gate, the Holocaust Memorial, and the Reichstag — a city-wide open-air museum of 20th-century history told in concrete, bronze, and memory. Try the Berlin swing, and explore Dark matter - a unique experience before heading out for another techno night-out.
Day 6 – Berlin to Prague
Transfer to Prague — a fairy-tale city of gothic spires, baroque palaces, and medieval bridges seemingly untouched by the modern world. Check in and take your first evening stroll across the Charles Bridge at dusk — one of Europe's most magical moments.
Day 7 – Castles, Bridges & Old Town
We start the day with a walking Tour with Local Guide: Wind through the labyrinthine cobblestone lanes of Malá Strana, across the Vltava River, and up to the commanding heights of Prague Castle. Watch the medieval Astronomical Clock strike the hour, surrounded by Gothic churches and Romanesque cellars turned into jazz bars.
Day 8 – Adventure & The Night Life
Wake up at your own time on this (free) day – those with adrenaline rush head out for a thrilling sky diving experience or take a short drive to the Skoda museum (any car lovers here?) or just walk around this pretty city and try out some amazing cafes (one of the cafes was frequented by Einstein) before an epic Pub Crawl in the night.
Day 9 – Prague to Budapest
Route: Prague → Budapest by bus The final leg of the overland journey sweeps south through the Czech and Slovak countryside before crossing into Hungary. Budapest — the grand old capital straddling the Danube — greets you with its dramatic Chain Bridge, thermal steam, and ruin bar culture.
Day 10 – On the Water & Into the Night
Explore the twin cities of Buda and Pest — stroll the UNESCO-listed Andrássy Avenue, visit the neo-Gothic Parliament Building on the Danube bank, and cross the iconic Chain Bridge between two distinct worlds. In the evening, cruise the Danube aboard a party boat — music, dancing, and the city's most romantic skyline all in one unforgettable evening.
Day 11 – Thermal Baths and Epic Send-Off
Széchenyi Thermal Bath: Soak in Budapest's most famous thermal spa — an opulent 1913 neo-baroque palace of steaming outdoor pools and indoor baths fed by natural geothermal springs. We regroup in the evening one last evening for some fun.
Day 12 – Departure from Budapest
A final morning in Budapest before transferring to the airport and flying home. Twelve days, four countries, four cities, and a lifetime of stories.
