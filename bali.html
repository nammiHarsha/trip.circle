Bali & Gili Island

 

Duration 9 Days
Group Size 15–20 people
Stay 3/4 star hotels
Trip Type Group Trip

About This Trip

From terraced rice paddies and ancient temples to the world's most legendary beach parties and underwater paradises — this is Bali and the Gili Islands at their absolute finest. Nine days of volcanic landscapes, spiritual culture, electric nightlife, turquoise waters, and island freedom across the Island of the Gods and its enchanting neighbouring archipelago.

Brief Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival in Bali
Touch down in Bali and transfer to your hotel in Seminyak. Freshen up and head to the Double Six beach for the sunset and we then have the Welcome Dinner with the group.
Day 2 – Surfing & Temples
Begin the morning with some surfing lessons at Kuta Beach. We then head to Tanah Lot / Uluwatu temple for those Insta worthy photos! In the evening, we will head out to one of the best clubs and party for learning surfing the waves!
Day 3 – Transfer to Gili Trawangan
We take a fast boat crossing to Gili Trawangan - no motorised vehicles exist on the island! Gili T's famous beachfront strip comes alive at night - Did I hear Tequila Sunrise?
Day 4 – Scuba / Snorkelling, Turtles & Beach Party
Wake to the sound of waves and begin the day with a scuba dive or snorkelling expedition in the crystal-clear waters surrounding the island. Rent a bicycle and circumnavigate the entire island - you can cover it in an hour! Put on some neon because we celebrate the first dive with the legendary Gili T beach party!
Day 5 – Transfer to Ubud
Transfer into the cultural heartland of Bali — Ubud by speedboat and a short journey through the charming rice terraces of Bali. Explore Ubud's charming art galleries, watch kecak show or just chill in the pool with the group - maybe sleep early today (pro-tip)
Day 6 – Sunrise Volcano Hike
Wake before dawn for a guided hike up Mount Batur. After the hike, unwind with a Balinese massage and we head out for an epic night out!
Day 7 – Cretya Lounge + ATV Rides
Visit Cretya lounge, overlooking Bali’s rice terraces. Later, enjoy a thrilling ATV ride through jungle trails, caves, and muddy tracks.
Day 8 – Beach Clubs & Farewell Night
Farewell to Ubud and make your way to Seminyak! Opt for a Nusa Penida day trip or relax before heading to Finns Beach Club for an unforgettable last night with squad!
Day 9 – Departure from Bali
Depart with sand still in your shoes, a camera full of memories, and the quiet certainty that Bali will call you back.
