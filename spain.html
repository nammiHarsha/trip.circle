Spain with Ibiza

 

Duration 11 Days
Group Size 15-20 people
Stay Hotels/Hostels
Trip Type Group Trip

About This Trip

From the biggest parties to ancient buildings, sun-drenched beaches to world-class cuisine — this trip captures the very best of Spain in one electrifying journey. Beginning at the beachside beauty of Barcelona and concluding in the grand capital of Madrid, with a detour to the legendary island of Ibiza and the charming city of Valencia along the way. Get ready to turn up the heat.

Brief Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival in Barcelona
Fly into Barcelona and check into your hostel — your base for the next three nights. Settle in, freshen up, and meet your travel squad before heading out to explore. Cap the evening with a Welcome Dinner that sets the tone for the ten extraordinary days ahead, followed by entry to one of Barcelona's most popular nightclubs — dancing and partying, Barcelona-style.
Day 2 – Bicycle Tour, Sagrada Família & Flamenco Masterclass
Begin the day with a guided bicycle tour through Barcelona's most iconic avenues and offbeat neighbourhoods. Next, stand before the extraordinary Sagrada Família. Step into the world of Spanish dance with a Flamenco Masterclass. In the evening, head to the Mediterranean shores for sangrias, swimming, and pure beachside bliss at Barcelona's most vibrant beach.
Day 3 – Costa Brava
The day spans the Costa Brava's old town and three distinct beaches. Spend the full day swimming, exploring, and soaking in the wild natural beauty of this storied coastline. Rent kayaks and paddle along the dramatic sea cliffs. Or, skydive over the same spectacular stretch of coastline, with a free-fall view that is simply incomparable.
Day 4 – Arrival in Ibiza & Nightlife
Travel to Ibiza by flight on Day 4. Day parties, world-renowned DJs, beach clubs, and warehouse bars — this island has earned its global reputation as the undisputed capital of good times. After checking into the hostel, head to a nearby beach for the most spectacular sunset on the island — Ibiza sunsets are legendary for good reason. After dinner, step out into the electric island nightlife, or rest and conserve energy knowing there are two more incredible nights ahead.
Day 5 – Beach & Boat Party
The morning begins with a swim session at one of Ibiza's most stunning beaches. Set sail on a sunset boat party with abundant drinks, epic DJ sets, and an unforgettable sunset over the Mediterranean while the party rages around you. Board a Disco Bus with the squad and party through the island's legendary clubs deep into the night.
Day 6 – Old Town & Pool Party
Explore the Ibiza Old Town (Dalt Vila) at your own pace. Put on your swimsuit and dive headfirst into the most iconic pool party in Ibiza — a daytime spectacle of music, energy, and sheer extravagance beneath the Ibizan sun. As the sun sets, head out to a club where world-class DJs take the stage.
Day 7 – Arrival in Valencia & Nightlife
After a long and scenic ferry crossing, we arrive in Valencia. Spend the first day exploring the city independently — wander the old quarter, discover the vast Turia Gardens. Later, experience the buzzing Valencian nightlife scene.
Day 8 – Guided Walking Tour & Malvarossa Beach
Begin the morning with a guided walking tour through Valencia's historic old town. Try traditional Valencian Paella for lunch — its resemblance to familiar flavours will surprise and delight in equal measure. Make the short journey to Malvarossa Beach on the Mediterranean coast. Take a dip in warm sea waters, play beach volleyball, or simply lie back and watch the sky dissolve into fifty shades of orange over the horizon.
Day 9 – Arrival in Madrid & Pub Crawl
A short, comfortable high-speed train journey south delivers the group into Madrid — the biggest city and the capital of Spain. Visit a panoramic vantage point offering stunning views across the entire city — Madrid at sunset is a sight to savour. Sample local tapas before heading out on the pub crawl — hopping through the city centre's most lively bars before ending the night at one of Madrid's most popular clubs.
Day 10 – City Walk, San Miguel Market & Santiago Bernabéu
Begin with a city walking tour accompanied by a Churro tasting experience at the legendary Mercado de San Miguel. Visit the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium — the iconic home of Real Madrid Football Club. Seek out a rooftop bar and raise a glass with the squad as Madrid glitters beneath the night sky.
Day 11 – Farewell
And just like that, it's over. Fly out of Madrid anytime on Day 11.
